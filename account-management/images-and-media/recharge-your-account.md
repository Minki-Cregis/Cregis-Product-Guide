# Recharge Your Account

**Please noted that the account fees** **could be** <mark style="color:green;">**only applied to the following situations**</mark>:

* Purchase team plan and additional feature for your team
* Purchase Tron energy (For more details, please refer to this "[**Tron Energy**](../../wallet-and-transactions/transaction/tron-energy-mode.md)".)



**You can follow the below steps to recharge your account.**

1.  Go to the account fees page.<br>

    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (18).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
2.  Click "<mark style="color:green;">**Recharge**</mark>", then a window with crypto address will be poped up.<br>

    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (19).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
3.  If you have recharged successfully, you can check your balance and details here.<br>

    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (20).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
