# Token

## **Cregis Desktop**

Wallet creators and team creators can set/add the virtual currency types of the wallet, including all currencies, popular, main chain, and stablecoins.

1.  Enter the token management page below.<br>

    <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (517).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
2.  You can view the token you added here and you can add more by "+" icon.<br>

    <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (518).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
3.  You can search the token by their name or type and click the "+" to add the token. You can just close the window after finishing adding the token.<br>

    <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (519).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
