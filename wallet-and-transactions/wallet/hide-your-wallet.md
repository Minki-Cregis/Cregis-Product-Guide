## Show Hidden Wallet <a href="#show-hidden-wallet" id="show-hidden-wallet"></a>

You can only show the hidden wallet in desktop version, you have to right-click on the blank space under "<mark style="color:green;">**Wallet**</mark>", then you can see the option to show hidden wallets.
